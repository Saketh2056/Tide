# 🌊 Tide — Reclaim your attention

Tide is an app for Android that helps you spend less time on your phone and more time on what
matters. Instead of just locking you out, it gives you a calm, gentle pause — a moment to stop
and ask, "do I really want to open this right now?"

It's simple to use, looks beautiful, and works completely on your phone. **It never connects to
the internet, has no ads, no accounts, and collects nothing about you.**

---

## What can Tide do?

Here is everything Tide offers, explained simply.

### 🛡 Shields — set limits on distracting apps

A "shield" protects you from an app that eats your time (like Instagram, YouTube, or a game).
For each app you can choose one of two options:

- **Time limit** — Give the app a daily budget, for example *30 minutes a day*. You can use it
  normally until the time runs out, then Tide gently pauses it for the rest of the day.
- **Full block** — Pause the app every single time you open it, all day. Good for apps you want
  to quit completely.

As your time runs low, Tide shows a small friendly reminder like **"5 minutes left on
Instagram"** so the limit never catches you by surprise.

### 🌙 Schedules — block apps during certain hours

A "schedule" turns apps off during the hours you choose, automatically. For example:

- A **wind-down** schedule that blocks social media every night from 10 PM to 6:30 AM so you
  sleep better.
- A **deep-work** schedule that blocks games every weekday morning.

You pick the start time, end time, and which days of the week it runs. Schedules that cross
midnight (like night-time ones) work perfectly.

### ✨ Focus sessions — distraction-free time when you need it

When you need to concentrate, start a focus session. Just pick how long (like 25 minutes),
choose which apps to silence, and tap start. During the session:

- A calm timer counts down with a gentle "breathing" animation.
- The apps you chose are blocked, so you can't get pulled away.
- If you try to quit early, you have to **hold the button down** for a moment — this stops you
  from giving up on impulse.
- When you finish, you get a little celebration. 🎉

Tide also keeps a history of your sessions so you can see your progress.

### 🧘 The Mindful Pause — the heart of Tide

Whenever an app is blocked, Tide doesn't just slam a door in your face. Instead it shows a calm
full-screen pause with a slow breathing circle and a kind message. It even shows how long you've
already used the app today.

If you really need to keep going, there's an **"Open anyway"** button — but it only appears
**after you take a slow breath** (a short countdown). You also only get a few of these "open
anyway" passes per day. This tiny bit of friction turns a mindless habit into a real choice.

### 📊 Today — your daily snapshot

The home screen shows your day at a glance:

- A big ring showing your total screen time compared to your daily goal.
- Whether you're using your phone **more or less than yesterday**.
- How many times you picked up your phone, how long you focused, and how many shields are active.
- Your most-used apps today, and a chart showing what times of day you were on your phone.

### 📈 Insights — your trends over time

The insights screen helps you see the bigger picture:

- A chart of your screen time over the **last 7 days**.
- Your **daily average** and your **streak** (how many days in a row you stayed under your goal).
- Which apps took up the most time this week.
- A slider to set your daily screen-time goal.
- A friendly, encouraging note based on how you're doing.

### 🎨 Looks and feel

- A guided **welcome tour** when you first open the app that helps you set everything up.
- **Dark, Light, or System** theme — your choice.
- Smooth animations and a clean, modern design throughout.

## Is it private and safe?

Yes. Tide is built to respect you completely:

- ✅ **No internet** — the app literally cannot send data anywhere. There's no internet
  permission at all.
- ✅ **No accounts, no sign-up, no ads, no tracking.**
- ✅ Everything stays **only on your phone.**
- ✅ It only sees *which* app is open so it can pause it — it **never reads what's on your
  screen.**

---

## What permissions does it need, and why?

When you first open Tide, it asks for two permissions and explains each one. Setup takes about
30 seconds.

| Permission | What it's for | Needed? |
|---|---|---|
| **Usage access** | Lets Tide measure your screen time so limits and charts work | Yes |
| **Accessibility (Tide Guardian)** | Lets Tide notice which app is open and show the pause screen | Yes |
| **Notifications** | A gentle nudge when a focus session finishes | Optional |

Each step in the app links you straight to the right settings screen and shows a green check
once it's done.

## How to install it

1. Copy the file **`Tide-1.0.0.apk`** to your Android phone.
2. Tap it to install. If your phone warns you, choose **"allow from this source"** — this is
   normal for apps installed outside the Play Store.
3. Open Tide and follow the short welcome tour to grant the two permissions.
4. Set up your first shield, schedule, or focus session — and enjoy a calmer phone. 🌊

> **Tip:** On some phones (like Xiaomi or Samsung), the battery saver can slow down apps like
> Tide. If blocking ever feels slow, turn off battery optimization for Tide in your phone's
> settings.
